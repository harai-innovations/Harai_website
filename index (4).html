PROJECT 01Independent projectHardware + ML

Non-invasive electrolyte monitoring band

A wearable that measures key electrolytes through the skin — sodium, potassium, chloride and calcium — using sweat/ISF biosensing. No blood pricking, no lab, continuous all day.

  • Measures Na⁺, K⁺, Cl⁻, Ca²⁺ continuously
  • Smart alerts on abnormal electrolyte levels
  • Lightweight, water-resistant, long battery life

Why HARAI

Most disease is detectable long before it's diagnosed. Each of our projects narrows that gap in its own domain — turning the signals people already carry into earlier, better-informed care.

We keep these efforts independent on purpose. A wearable sensor, an imaging model, a cardiac classifier, a neuro predictor and a records platform carry different risks, regulators and roadmaps — so they move at their own pace, with their own teams.

Sensor engineers, ML researchers and clinicians work under one quality system, so every project meets the same bar for validation, traceability and trust.
